Who We Are

About Urban View Plus

Delivering excellence in aluminium and metal fabrication across Ghana

Urban View Plus is your trusted partner for aluminium works, glass installation, and metal fabrication in Kumasi and beyond. We specialize in precision welding, custom gates, elegant railings, high-quality doors and windows, plus complete structural fabrication for residential and commercial projects.

From Alucobond cladding to burglary-proof installations and stainless balustrades, we combine craftsmanship with modern technology to deliver durable, aesthetic solutions that stand the test of time.
